## üé® Logo Guidelines

### Logo Usage

#### Primary Logo
The Qivook logo should be used in its full form whenever possible, maintaining clear space around it.

**Clear Space**: Minimum clear space around the logo should be equal to the height of the "Q" in Qivook.

#### Logo Variations

1. **Full Logo (Horizontal)**
   - Preferred for most applications
   - Use on light backgrounds
   - Minimum width: 120px

2. **Full Logo (Vertical/Stacked)**
   - Use when horizontal space is limited
   - Minimum height: 80px

3. **Icon Only**
   - Use when space is extremely limited
   - Minimum size: 32px √ó 32px
   - Always include clear space around it

4. **Monochrome Versions**
   - Use on colored backgrounds
   - Available in white and black versions

#### Logo Placement

**DO:**
- ‚úÖ Place logo in top-left corner of websites/apps
- ‚úÖ Use on white or light backgrounds (primary logo)
- ‚úÖ Maintain clear space around logo
- ‚úÖ Use appropriate size for context

**DON'T:**
- ‚ùå Stretch or distort the logo
- ‚ùå Rotate the logo
- ‚ùå Add effects (shadows, gradients, outlines) to the logo
- ‚ùå Place logo on busy backgrounds without proper contrast
- ‚ùå Use logo smaller than minimum sizes

#### Logo Colors

- **Primary**: Use brand primary color (#1E3A8A for Construction, #166534 for Agriculture)
- **Monochrome**: Black (#1F2937) or White (#FFFFFF)
- **Grayscale**: Use for single-color applications

All color combinations must meet WCAG 2.1 Level AA standards:
- **Normal text**: 4.5:1 contrast ratio minimum
- **Large text (18pt+)**: 3:1 contrast ratio minimum
- **UI components**: 3:1 contrast ratio minimum

Use tools like [WebAIM Contrast Checker](https://webaim.org/resources/contrastchecker/) to verify.

## üí¨ Tone of Voice

### Brand Voice Characteristics

1. **Friendly & Approachable**
   - Use "you" and "your" to speak directly to users
   - Avoid jargon; explain technical terms
   - Use emojis strategically (not excessively)

2. **Empowering & Positive**
   - Focus on benefits and outcomes
   - Use action-oriented language
   - Celebrate user achievements

3. **Clear & Direct**
   - Short sentences
   - Active voice
   - Specific, concrete language

4. **Regional & Authentic**
   - Use East African context and examples
   - Reference local currencies (KES, UGX, TZS, RWF)
   - Acknowledge regional challenges and opportunities

### Writing Guidelines

#### Headlines
- **DO**: "Your Trade Adventure Starts Here!" üöÄ
- **DON'T**: "Welcome to the Qivook Platform"

#### Body Text
- **DO**: "Track prices across 4 countries in real-time"
- **DON'T**: "Our platform enables real-time price tracking functionality across multiple East African countries"

#### CTAs (Call-to-Actions)
- **DO**: "Start Your Free Trial", "Explore Suppliers", "Get Started"
- **DON'T**: "Click Here", "Submit", "Continue"

#### Error Messages
- **DO**: "Oops! We couldn't find that supplier. Try searching with a different name."
- **DON'T**: "Error 404: Supplier not found"

#### Success Messages
- **DO**: "üéâ Great! Your price alert has been created."
- **DON'T**: "Operation completed successfully"

### Emoji Usage

**Strategic Use:**
- ‚úÖ Headers and hero sections
- ‚úÖ Success messages
- ‚úÖ Feature highlights
- ‚úÖ Navigation icons (sparingly)

**Avoid:**
- ‚ùå Professional emails
- ‚ùå Error messages
- ‚ùå Legal/terms text
- ‚ùå Overuse (max 1-2 per section)

## üì∏ Imagery & Photography

### Photography Style

#### Preferred Style
- **Authentic**: Real people, real businesses, real East African contexts
- **Bright & Optimistic**: Well-lit, positive atmosphere
- **Professional but Approachable**: Business settings that feel accessible
- **Diverse**: Represent different countries, industries, and people

#### Subjects
- Construction sites and materials
- Agricultural fields and farmers
- Business professionals in offices
- Marketplaces and trading centers
- Technology in use (tablets, phones, computers)
- East African landscapes and cityscapes

#### Technical Specifications
- **Format**: High-resolution JPEG or PNG
- **Resolution**: Minimum 1920√ó1080 for web, 300 DPI for print
- **Aspect Ratios**: 16:9 (hero), 4:3 (cards), 1:1 (thumbnails)
- **File Size**: Optimized for web (< 500KB when possible)

### Stock Photography Guidelines

**DO:**
- ‚úÖ Use authentic-looking stock photos
- ‚úÖ Choose images that reflect East African context
- ‚úÖ Ensure diversity in representation
- ‚úÖ Use images that support the content

**DON'T:**
- ‚ùå Use generic, overly staged photos
- ‚ùå Use images that don't match East African context
- ‚ùå Use low-quality or pixelated images
- ‚ùå Use images with watermarks

### Image Treatment

#### Overlays
Use subtle dark overlays (20-40% opacity) on hero images to improve text readability.

#### Filters
Avoid heavy filters. Use subtle adjustments:
- Slight brightness increase (+5-10%)
- Subtle contrast boost (+5%)
- Warm color temperature (slight)
