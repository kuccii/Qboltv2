### General Questions

**Q: What is Qivook?**
A: Qivook is a trade intelligence platform designed for East African businesses. We provide real-time price tracking, verified supplier networks, logistics planning, and trade financing solutions - all in one platform.

**Q: Who is Qivook for?**
A: Qivook is designed for:
- Construction companies
- Agriculture businesses
- Traders and importers/exporters
- Logistics providers
- Anyone involved in cross-border trade in East Africa

**Q: Which countries does Qivook cover?**
A: Currently, Qivook covers Kenya, Uganda, Tanzania, and Rwanda. We're expanding to more countries in the region.

**Q: How much does Qivook cost?**
A: Qivook offers a free trial for 14 days. After that, plans start at $79/month for basic features. Enterprise plans are available for larger organizations. Contact us for custom pricing.

### Price Tracking Questions

**Q: How accurate are the prices on Qivook?**
A: Qivook tracks prices from multiple sources including verified suppliers, market reports, and user submissions. All prices are verified and updated in real-time. We maintain 95%+ accuracy across all tracked materials.

**Q: How often are prices updated?**
A: Prices are updated in real-time as new data becomes available. On average, we process 10,000+ price updates daily across all materials and regions.

**Q: Can I set price alerts?**
A: Yes! You can set price alerts for any material. You'll receive notifications via email or in-app when prices drop to your target level.

**Q: What materials can I track?**
A: Qivook tracks prices for:
- Construction materials (cement, steel, timber, sand, etc.)
- Agricultural inputs (fertilizer, seeds, pesticides, etc.)
- Trade commodities

### Supplier Questions

**Q: How do you verify suppliers?**
A: We verify suppliers through:
- Document verification (business licenses, certifications)
- Transaction history review
- Customer reviews and ratings
- Insurance status checks
- Regular audits

**Q: How many suppliers are on Qivook?**
A: Qivook has 2,500+ verified suppliers across East Africa, covering construction and agriculture industries.

**Q: Can I contact suppliers directly through Qivook?**
A: Yes! You can message suppliers directly through the platform, view their contact information, and even request quotes.

**Q: Are suppliers required to pay to be listed?**
A: Suppliers can create a basic profile for free. Premium listings with enhanced features are available for a subscription fee.

### Technical Questions

**Q: Do I need to download an app?**
A: Qivook is available as a web application and mobile app (iOS and Android). You can access it from any device with an internet connection.

**Q: Is my data secure?**
A: Yes! We use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your data. We're compliant with data protection regulations and never share your information with third parties without consent.

**Q: Can I integrate Qivook with my existing systems?**
A: Yes! Qivook offers API access for enterprise customers. Contact us to discuss integration options.

**Q: What if I need help using Qivook?**
A: We offer comprehensive help documentation, video tutorials, and email support. Enterprise customers also get dedicated account management.
